Week Four Questions

What was one thing you liked and disliked about each of the new topics covered in Prework?

I'm not too sure which topics you're referring to, but if its HTML/CSS/Javascript:
I liked understanding more of the interaction between HTML and CSS. I still don't like the actual design process, involving adding css and then visually checking the page, changing the css again, etc.
I really enjoyed working with javascript. Writing code that's simple and does the job is extremely satisfying. I didn't dislike anything about it. Though I am told that once I learn other languages I will have things to complain about with javascript.

What was helpful?

Justin and Ian were quite helpful! When I asked a question on slack, they responded within the minute.

What hindered your progress?

I can't think of anything external that really hindered me. Wow classic, perhaps.

Blind persistence.

First Post

August 30th, 2019

Was your first week what you expected? Why? Why not?

The first week wasn't too surprising for me. Meeting on the first day was interesting, it was strange to be in a classroom or school-like environment with much older students (including me) than the last time I was in school.

What are you struggling with?

Well, for the this assignment in particular I managed to tunnel on one slide and forget to check the others for assignments. I have also missed a standup purely due to my own negligence. These types of logistical struggles are common for me, so I will just need to stay on full alert for the duration of the prework. Once the in-person work begins, I expect to have less problems in that area.

What are you excited or eager to learn more about?

I'm excited to get more into javascript, node, react, etc. The logical elements behind webpages are way more interesting to me than the visual ones.
